Services

Our services helps your organization to run OpenSlides professional and successfully on your assembly.

All services are offerd by Intevation GmbH – a Free Software company from Osnabrück which cooperates closely with the OpenSlides community and supports the development and maintenance of OpenSlides.

Customizing

Get the OpenSlides projector in your corporate design. Enhance your OpenSlides application with new or customized features.

Hosting

Use OpenSlides as online service without installation effort! We host your customized OpenSlides installation on our powerful server infrastructur – secure and reliable.

Individual support

Get help to configure, use and understand the power of OpenSlides. We provide telephone and email support to assist and advise you in the preparation phase.

Training

Train your staff members in a workshop. We show you how to best integrate OpenSlides in your assembly.


 

On-site support

Book a technician who assist you on-site in setting up and using Openslides while your assembly.

Electronic voting

Rent a digital electronic voting system (TED system) with keypads to accelerate the voting process of motions and elections.
